ECHO VI: COLLAGE

Our writing is a collage of our beliefs, observations, dreams, and imagination. We weave these fragments of ourselves into even the most formulaic lines or brushstrokes, and that makes our works uniquely “us”. Our magazine, then, is a collage of voices – quiet or loud, brisk or flamboyant, serious or humorous – under each of which hides a unique personality. It is a cacophony. But in a good way.

And we want your *voice* in our noisy collage. It can be anything – short stories, poems, essays, op-eds, artworks, photographs, hermit crabs, word-salads. There are no restrictions, no required topics, no “other *matters*.” Please send us anything that is authentically you.

JOIN US

After we collect the works from our authors and artists, we need to piece the collage pieces together. To clarify and make full potential of each voice, we need to carefully trim and decorate each piece. After that, we need to paste all of the pieces together and add the finishing touches.

Editors and formatters are essential to making our perfect collage. Editors proofread the literary works for better expression, so as to sharpen the meaning of each piece. Formatters decide on the presentation of each work, arrange the various pieces together, and add miscellaneous pages such as credits and contents.
